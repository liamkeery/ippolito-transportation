Our Priority

Safety At Ippolito

Load up a truck, hop into the cab and head off to your destination; what could be more simple than that? However, the reality of a trucking job is a lot more complex than that. Few other jobs require anything near the level of planning, logistics and safety measures that are required for driving truck and everything associated with trucking industry.

Our Safety Checklist

Safety Is Everyone's Concern

Safety is a job we take seriously. We are fully committed to the safety of our drivers, our employees, the public, our terminals, the environment, our equipment, and your freight. Ippolito Transportation strives to provide a healthy, injury-free workplace through proactive prevention programs.

  • Employee lead Transportation Joint Health & Safety Committee (JHSC)
  • Road Testing for all Company Driver and Owner Operators
  • Company training (Example; WHIMIS, Workplace Violence, Sexual Harassment, & more)
  • Proactive employee surveys with action items for any area of improvement
  • Company Driver and Owner Operator have 24*7 access to Operations team
  • Regular equipment inspections, with repairs done at our onsite shop
  • We provide drivers with regular refresher training

Safety In All Lanes

Road Safety is one of the biggest concerns in the trucking industry

Everyone at Ippolito Transportation is aware of road safety. Our Logistic Coordinators listen when a driver voices a concern. We have system in place for proactive tracking of the weather, however we understand nothing is more reliable than boots on the ground. Our Company Drivers and Owner Operators make the call, in real time, as road and route conditions change.

Our in-house Mechanics pride themselves on well maintained fleet. All feedback from Company Drivers and Owner Operators is addressed immediately. Ippolito Transportation takes your safety seriously.

Giving Back

Community Support

At Ippolito Transportation Inc. we believe in giving back to the community and to contribute to the quality of life in the communities we serve. Ippolito Transportation Inc. has been, and continues to be, a community minded organization with a tradition of family involvement and a responsible corporate attitude.

Ippolito Transportation Inc. is always looking for ways to give back to the community and donations are one of the ways we accomplish this. It feels good to do good. All of the donations raised stay in the community where they were raised to benefit local registered charities.

Our Commitment

Giving Tree

We Care, formerly known as Friends of We Care, unites a network of Member Sponsor organizations and industry supporters with one common goal: bringing joy to kids with disabilities. As the Charity of Choice of the Foodservice and Hospitality Industry, we partner with companies to raise money through fundraising events and initiatives to send kids with disabilities to fully accessible Easter Seals summer camps across Canada.
