Less-than-Truckload Solutions (LTL)

Ippolito Transportation, we specialize in cross-border temp-controlled freight. We “get” food and we “get” produce because we were created two decades ago to serve our own sister produce company. We know how to deliver for customers demanding the strictest protocols on quality control and food safety. We are asset-based with a focus on refrigerated equipment and facilities offering maximum flexibility, security, and control. We also do 3PL with a network of longstanding trusted carrier partners. We do move refrigerated and dry LTL in all verticals.

At Ippolito Transportation, we are committed to providing you the best service in the industry 24X7, 364 days a day. To accomplish this, we have established a Logistics Coordinators team of highly trained professionals dedicated to responding to your needs and delivering superior value.

Consolidate less-than-truckload (LTL) provides cost effective solutions. Our rates are based on load consolidation to reduce your LTL costs. We do not price on density or class. Shipping made simple! Ippolito Transportation, we pride ourselves in reducing complexity for our customers.

What We Offer

  • Canada and USA coverage
