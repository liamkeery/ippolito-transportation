Ippolito Flatbed Solutions

When it comes to specialized commodities like heavy equipment and construction materials, or simply loads that are larger than a van or require crane-loading, Ippolito Transportation has a Flatbed division to meet your needs. Our team works with 3PL exclusively for all your Flatbed freight for safe, timely, and secure delivery.

At Ippolito Transportation, we provide our customers with access to flatbed capacity anywhere in Canada and United States. We understand that requirements differ for each customer. Shipping made simple!
